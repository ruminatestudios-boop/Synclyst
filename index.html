[ faq ]

Got questions?

What can I scan with AutoEntry?

Receipts, invoices, product labels, IDs, and most documents with structured data. We support images and PDFs. Our AI detects the type and extracts the relevant fields automatically.

Which apps do you connect to?

We’re adding 200+ connectors: Shopify, QuickBooks, Xero, Stripe, Notion, Airtable, Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Sheets, Slack, and more. You connect them from your phone with one tap—no desktop required. See the Connectors section for current status.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don’t train models on your data. You own your data and can delete it or export it at any time.

How does the free trial work?

You get 5 free scans on your phone with no signup. After that, connect one app (e.g. Google Sheets) from your phone to get 10 more free scans. When you’re ready, upgrade to Pro for unlimited scans and all connectors.

What happens after I join the waitlist?

You’ll get a link to open the scanner on your phone. Use your 5 free scans, connect an app to unlock more, or upgrade for unlimited. We’re there for setup and any questions.
