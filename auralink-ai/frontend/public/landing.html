    How it works

    From photo to live listing in 4 steps.

    Do this to get your products on Shopify—no typing required.

    1

    Snap or upload a photo

    Take a picture of your product or upload one from your device. One photo is enough to get started.

    2

    Review your draft listing

    AI fills in title, description, category, and tags. Check the result and edit anything you want before pushing to your store.

    3

    Push to Shopify as draft

    Send the listing to your Shopify store as a draft. It won’t go live until you say so—you’re in control.

    4

    Publish when you’re ready

    In Shopify admin, review the draft, make any final tweaks, then hit Publish. Your product is live and ready for buyers.
