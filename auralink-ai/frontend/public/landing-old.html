    [ faq ]

    Got questions?

    What does SyncLyst extract from a product image?

    Attributes (material, color, weight, dimensions, brand), SEO title, high-converting description, bullet points, category, and search keywords. We use OCR for labels (e.g. 100% Cotton) and VLMs for visual features.

    Does this work on mobile?

    Yes. The site is fully mobile-responsive and the landing page supports in-browser camera capture and photo upload on phones and tablets. Snap a product with your camera, get your draft listing in seconds, then push to your Shopify store—all from your device. Etsy, eBay, and more coming soon. No desktop required.

    Which marketplaces do you sync to?

    Right now we sync to Shopify. Etsy, eBay, TikTok Shop, Amazon, and other integrations are coming soon. One master profile, with more platform adapters on the way.

    How fast is extraction?

    Target <3 seconds from photo upload to draft listing. We use Gemini 2.0 Flash or GPT-4o for vision, with optional OCR enrichment.

    Is my product data secure?

    Yes. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't train models on your images or listings. You own your data and can export or delete it at any time.

    Can I edit the extraction before syncing?

    Absolutely. Every extraction becomes a draft in the Dashboard. Edit title, description, attributes, and tags before pushing to any channel.

    What image formats and types work?

    We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP. Works with packaged products, unpackaged items, handmade goods, and even screenshots of listings. Clear, well-lit photos give the best results.

    What is Audit Mode (Power User)?

    For images with multiple items—e.g. a box of 50 patches—Audit Mode estimates the count and highlights each unit with bounding boxes so you can confirm before listing. Built for bulk inventory and high-volume sellers.

    How do I get started?

    Sign up for the Dashboard, then try a product scan from your phone or upload an image from the dashboard. No credit card needed for the free tier.
